Cockroach Management Service


Cockroaches are probably the worst household pest that can be dealt with as they come in search of food and hide well unless a good population is present in your house. They hide in places wherever they fit and also infect leftover food, causing many diseases. This pest can be removed in earlier stages by these solutions.

Cockroaches adapt well to residential or commercial properties and are extremely hardy creatures.
How to Know If You Have Cockroach Infestation:

  • 1. Faecal residue
  • 2. Foul odour especially in damp and dark areas
  • 3. Egg cases
  • 4. Body parts usually limbs

Treatment:

Gel and Spray Treatment: Bayer gel is applied in closed cabinet areas, in tables, door hinges and drawers. An odorless spray will be sprayed in all open areas such as window frames, balconies, bathrooms, etc.


Cockroach Prevention Tips:
  1. Proof Entry Point – Cracks and cracks around the sink, window and electrical windows and make sure the kitchen wall and floor tiles are well numbered.
  2. Store food items appropriately – use Tupperware or metal containers where possible, with a tightly fitting lid.
  3. Good Housekeeping – Clean up leftover food, sweep away any food scraps and mop the spillage immediately. Clean and clean all surfaces and floors regularly, removing all food residue.
  4. Eliminate clutter – Take supplies from locked boxes and stores in cupboards and open metal shelving. This will reduce the harbor areas for cockroaches and aid visual inspections.
